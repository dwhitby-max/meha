01 — Purpose

A gathering, not a conference.

The question isn't whether medicine is changing. It is who gets to shape what it becomes — and how we live well while it does.

MEHA convenes leading physicians, researchers, and emerging healthcare stakeholders for three days of exchange at the oceanfront Eau Resort & Spa in Palm Beach. Attendees hear from world-renowned experts and explore breakthrough science in oncology, cell therapy, and longevity.

The program culminates in the Starry Night Gala and a round at a premier Palm Beach County golf course — a community of up to 350 guests, by invitation, committed to advancing our mission: Make Everyone Healthy Again.
